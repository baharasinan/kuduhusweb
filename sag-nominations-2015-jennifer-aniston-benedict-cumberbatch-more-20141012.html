SAG Nominations 2015: Jennifer Aniston, Benedict Cumberbatch, More

Who does Hollywood want? Excitement for the 2015 Awards season continued to build on Wednesday, Dec. 10, with the announcement of the nominees for the 21st Annual Screen Actors Guild Awards. The 2015 nods were revealed in a live telecast featuring Fault in Our Stars hunk Ansel Elgort and actress Eva Longoria.

The nominees are selected by 4,200 SAG members each year, including 2,100 members who select honorees in film, and the other half in television. The selection process for each category will then be made by all 4,200 members.

On Sunday, Jan. 25, television and film’s biggest stars will convene inside L.A.’s Shrine Auditorium and laud the achievements made by their peers this past year. Check out the full list of nominees below and tell Us: Who will you be rooting for?
