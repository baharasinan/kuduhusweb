Who is The Quad actor Jake Allyn's girlfriend?

Jake Allyn is an American actor and writer who first gained major attention after playing BoJohn Folsom in the BET series The Quad. He also appeared in movies like 2015's Appetites and 2016's Drone wars.

Allyn currently is in the spotlight because of the film No Man's Land, which he wrote, and produced.

Jake Allyn- Wiki-Bio

Jake Allyn was born on June 10, 1990, and as of the writing of this article, he is 30-years-of age. He grew up in Texas with his family, which consists of his father, mother, and four years older brother Conor Allyn.

Jake Allyn with his brother, Conor Allyn.
Image Source: Variety

Allyn, in various interviews, has mentioned that his father, Ron Allyn, who is a political consultant by profession, played a major role in installing passion for movies in him and his brother. From a young age, their father used to take them to watch classic movies such as Braveheart despite not being child-friendly.

Jake Allyn- Career and Net Worth

Jake Allyn and his brother, Conor, grew up making home videos on their parents' camcorder. After college graduation, the Allyn brothers and their father started a small production company titled Margate House Films and began making feature films.
