Mia Tyler is an American actress, model, fashion designer, music promoter, talent manager, author, media personality, visual artist, and socialite who first gained popularity for being the daughter of rock singer Steven Tyler and actress Cyrinda Foxe. She has appeared in several TV shows and films such as House of Style, Celebrity Fit Club, A Little Bit of Lipstick, Rush Hour 3, and Hell’s Kitchen. As a model, Mia has done modeling work for brands like H&M and MXM and has been featured in a variety of magazines such as Seventeen, Teen, Us, Teen People, Mode, Jump, Vogue, and Flare. Mia is also the owner of her clothing line, Revolution 1228, and she released an autobiography called Creating Myself in 2008.
