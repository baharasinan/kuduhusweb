Who Is Lucy Mendez, Ace Steel Wife? Age And Wikipedia

Lucy Mendez, famous to many as the wife of Ace Steel, has carved out her own significant legacy in the world of professional wrestling as a talented wrestler.

They have again been making headlines due to their involvement in a previous backstage altercation in 2022.

Christopher Guy, born on January 25, 1973, is an American professional wrestler famous by his ring name, Ace Steel.

He gained significant recognition for his contributions to Ring of Honor and occasional World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) appearances.

Additionally, Guy had engagements with WWE’s Ohio Valley Wrestling developmental territory and held positions as a backstage producer in All Elite Wrestling on two separate occasions.

His passion for professional wrestling ignited during childhood while attending American Wrestling Association shows at the International Amphitheatre.

Following this interest, Guy debuted in October 1991 after receiving training from Windy City Pro Wrestling.

Ace Steel Wife, Lucy Mendez

Lucy Mendez, recognized in the wrestling world as the spouse of Ace Steel, is a prominent female wrestler hailing from the United States, born on October 12, 1982.

She has showcased her skills throughout her career across numerous independent promotions nationwide.

Mendez is a three-time World League Wrestling Ladies Champion.

Her contributions to the Missouri Wrestling Revival (MWR) are noteworthy, as evidenced by her two-time recognition as the MWR Female Wrestler of the Year.

Additionally, within MWR, Lucy played pivotal managerial roles for both Ace Steel and Elvis Aliaga.

Her tenure at this Missouri-centric promotion reached a pinnacle in 2016 when she won the MWR Manager of the Year title.

Recently, Stephen P. New, the legal representative for CM Punk and Ace Steel, highlighted Lucy Mendez in an interview. New emphasized,

“The only person, and granted she’s not completely objective and unbiased, the only person not subject to an NDA and not subject to confidentiality provisions is Ace’s wife Lucy.”

Chris Jericho Responds To CM Punk’s/ Ace Steel’s Attorney

Following the 2022 backstage scandal that shook the promotion, Stephen P. New, the attorney who represented Ace Steel and CM Punk after the AEW Brawl Out incident, engaged in a public debate on X (formerly known as Twitter) with Chris Jericho.

Their heated exchange revolves around New’s assertion that Ace Steel’s wife was the sole individual involved in the backstage controversy who did not sign a post-incident confidentiality agreement.

This prompted Jericho to disclose that he witnessed the events of that night but had not signed any Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs).

New referenced AEW’s employee handbooks, highlighting their “confidentiality provisions,” and claimed possession of such an agreement. In response, Jericho refuted New’s statement, challenging him to verify the actual facts.

The specifics of the incident remain elusive, but reports suggest a physical altercation occurred involving CM Punk, Ace Steel, Kenny Omega, and The Young Bucks. Consequently, the involved parties faced suspensions, with Steel even losing his position.

Although details remain scarce due to the tight-lipped nature of those involved, Jericho and New’s differing accounts hint at varying interpretations of the events.

Jericho further emphasized his firsthand perspective, stating,

“Because I saw EVERYTHING that night…including how Lucy (and her husband and best buddy) acted and what really went down…and since I was in the room and watching her and everybody else the whole time.”
