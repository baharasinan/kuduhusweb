Frank Gotti Death: How Did He Die?

Frank Gotti died at the young age of just 12 years old, and the cause of his death is an accident. 

Frank was hit by a car when he was on a mini-bike, which led to his death, but things are a bit complicated than just this. 

Frank was with his friends, and one of them had a mini-bike. The guy with the mini-bike was letting his friends use the bike in turn. 

However, as you might expect the son of a gangster to do, Frank took his friend’s bike and started using it without any proper instructions and started moving towards the road. 

There, he was hit by the car of his neighbor John Favara, who was riding the car. Despite he died because of the accident, John was let free because he had no fault in the accident. 

Frank died that day, but things became difficult for John. As expected, he disappeared a few days later and then was never found. 

Well, he was declared dead but no proof of that is yet found. Some say that he was dismembered and mixed into concrete, whereas some say that he was first killed and then melted with acid. 

Interestingly, Frank Gotti’s Gambino family had already left for Florida about 3 days before John’s disappearance.

Frank Gotti Siblings: Who Are John Gotti Children?

Frank Gotti was not the only child of his gangster father John Gotti. 

Frank’s father was married to his wife Victoria DiGiorgio for 4 decades, and in these years of togetherness, they gave birth to 5 children. 

Their eldest child, daughter Angel Gotti is now 60, their second child John Gotti Jr. is 57 now and he ran the crime family as a gangster after their father went to prison. 

The middle child was Frank, who passed away at just 12, and Frank had 2 other younger siblings, sister Victoria Gotti and brother Peter Gotti, Jr.

For a tribute to the lost Gambino family member who passed away so young, his siblings named one of their children Francis, in the memories of their lost uncle.
