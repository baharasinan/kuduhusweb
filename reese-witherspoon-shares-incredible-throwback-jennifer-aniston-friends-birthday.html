Reese Witherspoon shares incredible throwback of Jennifer Aniston for special celebration

Remember when Reese starred in Friends?

    It's Jennifer Aniston's birthday and to celebrate Reese Witherspoon shared a brilliant throwback of the time she appeared as a guest star on the hit sitcom .
