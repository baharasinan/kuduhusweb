Jaime King Debuts Post-Baby Body Two Weeks After Giving Birth to Son

Welcome back, Jaime! Jaime King stepped out for an afternoon outing in Beverly Hills, Calif., on Friday, Oct. 18, for the first time since giving birth to her child — baby boy James Knight — just two weeks ago. The Hart of Dixie actress showed off her post-baby body while grabbing some coffee with her mom Nancy at Urth Caffe. 

The 34-year-old first-time mom tried to keep a low profile during the outing, wearing tight black pants, an oversized white sweater and black shades.
