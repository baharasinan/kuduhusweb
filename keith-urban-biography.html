Keith Urban Bio (Wiki)

Who is Keith Urban?

Keith Urban is a famous New Zealand-born Australian-American country musician.

He is also famous as a singer, songwriter, guitarist, and record producer.

He won a 2012 Golden Globe Award for Best Original Song For You, which was featured in the movie Act of Valor.

Keith Urban: Birth Facts, Family Background, Ethnicity, Nationality

Keith was born on 26 October 1967, in Whangarei, North Island, New Zealand.

He is an American and Australian nationality and is of mixed ethnicity(English, German, Scottish, Irish).

His birth name is Keith Lionel Urban, he is the youngest son of Marienne Urban and Robert Urban.

Keith Urban has a brother named Shane Urban. He was raised by his parents in Caboolture, Queensland, Australia.

Education History

He was educated at Sir Edmund Hillary College in Otara, South Auckland.

Keith was interested in music since his childhood age and took lessons from his teacher, Sue McCarthy at a very young age.

He also performed at country festivals from the age of 10. In 1983, he participated in the Australian TV talent show New Faces.

Keith Urban: Professional Life, Career, Awards

Keith began his professional music career with regular appearances on the Reg Lindsay Country Homestead TV Program.

Shortly, he began appearing on various other music programs.

After that, he performed a duet with local Brisbane girl Jenny Wilson and won a golden guitar award at the Tamworth Country Music Festival.

Keith Urban also performed regularly on stage at the Northern Suburbs Country Music Club near Caboolture.

In 1990, Urban signed with EMI in Australia and released his self-titled debut album.

He moved to Nashville, Tennessee in 1992 and appeared in the music video for Alan Jackson’s rendition of “Mercury Blues”.

Keith co-wrote “Jesus Gets Jealous of Santa Claus” on the 1995 album Christmas to Christmas. He released his self-titled American debut album in 1999.

He released his second American album Golden Road in 2002. She has also made appearances as a Judge on American Idol.

Keith won the Academy of Country Music Awards- Top New Male Vocalist in 2001. He also won ARIA Music Award that same year.

Keith Urban won TCMA Awards for “Somebody Like You” in 2003. The following year, he won the Country Music Association Awards- Male Vocalist of the Year.
