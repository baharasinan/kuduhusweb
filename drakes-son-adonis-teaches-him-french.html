Drakes son Adonis teaches him French in adorable video

Multiple award-winning Canadian rapper Drake is extremely lucky – because he has a child who can switch tongues within seconds.

The God’s Plan rapper has shared an adorable video of his son teaching him how to speak French.

In the new video shared on the rapper’s Instagram, sighted by Ghanafuo.com, his son, Adonis Graham, is seen attempting to teach his father how to master the French language.

And he did it in a polite manner. While having a chit-chat, Adonis politely asked:  Can I teach you how to speak Français?

When Drake permitted him, he went on to drop a few sentences in French and translated to him.

We are unsure what he said, however, from the way he spoke, he sounds brilliant and fluent in the language.

Drake shares a special bond with Adonis, who is 4 years old.

Last month, the rapper shared a sweet video of his, showing off his impressive basketball skills.

In the video, which Drake shared on his Instagram Story, Adonis dribbles a basketball multiple times before effortlessly tossing the ball up into the hoop.

After making the shot, Adonis smiles at the camera and is met with cheers from his audience watching on the court. He also gets a big hug from his friend, who is playing basketball alongside him.
