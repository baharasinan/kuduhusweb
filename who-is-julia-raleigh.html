Julia Raleigh's Profile, Net Worth, Age, Height, Relationships, FAQs

With various lip-sync videos uploaded to her "@juliaaraleigh" account, Julia has amassed over 3.6 million fans. She became well-known for combining humor, dance, lip-syncing, and transitional videos on the platform.

When she was eleven years old, she started creating videos, primarily music videos that she would create on her mother's computer. Growing up, she gained a great deal of notoriety and acclaim for her vlogs, comedy videos, challenges, and practical jokes that she posted on her channel, Jules and Saud. She first became well-known on Vine, where on May 29, 2014, she uploaded her debut Vine video, "23 Miley Cyrus Choreography."

On May 26, 2014, Julia and her boyfriend Saud started a YouTube channel called Jules and Saud. This channel has over 2.7 million subscribers and the couple posts pranks, challenges, reactions, comedy videos, and vlogs.

After that, Julia started her own YouTube channel on October 22, 2019. She has amassed almost 102K subscribers there. Her initial attempt at stardom came after a joke video she made with her lover, A1saud, became viral online. Their channel has continued to expand quickly ever since.
