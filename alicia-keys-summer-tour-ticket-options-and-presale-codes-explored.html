Alicia Keys Summer Tour: Ticket Options and Presale Codes Explored

Alicia Keys will be hitting the road again after the conclusion of her ongoing world tour. The singer has announced a 23-date North American tour titled Keys to the Summer. The tour will kick off at the end of June in Fort Lauderdale, Fort Lauderdale, and move across cities like Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Washington DC, Salt Lake City, Oakland, and Austin before concluding in Los Angeles in August.

The tour will feature a completely redesigned production and a 360-degree stage. “The Keys to the Summer tour is going to be completely unforgettable and unmissable,” the singer said in a statement announcing the tour.

“I describe it as a celebration of freedom and self-liberation! It’s going to be colorful, magical and will light up your body, soul, and all of your senses! This is my first time creating a 360 experience and we’ve made sure it’s like nothing you’ve ever seen! I’m so excited to see y’all this summer!” she added.

Keys to the Summer Tour: Presale Code and Tickets

A presale for the tour concerts has already begun on April 18th at 10 am local time. Fans can use the presale code KEYS2THESUMMER to avail of the tickets. Live Nation will also be hosting a presale beginning Thursday, April 20th at 10 am local time. The code for the sale is VINYL.

Different venues will also be hosting individual presales, each with different codes, which can be checked out on their official websites. For example, TD Garden in Boston has the code GIRLONFIRE. The general tickets will go on sale on Friday, April 21st at 9 am local time via Ticketmaster.
