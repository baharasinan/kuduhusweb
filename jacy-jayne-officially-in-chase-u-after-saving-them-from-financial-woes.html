Jacy Jayne Officially Joins Chase U After Saving Them From Financial Woes

Chase U has been one of the most popular factions in NXT. Ever since their inception, they have been one of the most entertaining acts on television.

As popular as they are, Chase U came into some trouble recently due to a scandal that left them on the brink of bankruptcy. Andre Chase has tried everything to save the university but all plans seemed to backfire.

During the January 30th episode of WWE NXT, Andre Chase, Duke Hudson, and Riley Osborne gathered in the ring to say their goodbye to Chase U. Chase stated that the memories they made during their time together will remain forever.

Duke Hudson even got a neat tribute video put together showcasing all their good times over the years. Chase said that he is looking forward to their future. Just then Jacy Jayne came out with Thea Hail.

Jayne proclaimed to have a solution that would solve their problems. Jacy announced the ladies of Chase U calendar. A bunch of beautiful women came out to the ring. Jacy Jayne then said that the calendar will go live on 2/4 and will not only save them from their financial woes but will propel them to the next level. Chase U was so excited he was jumping and thanking Jacy. He then hugged her and told her that she is now officially part of Chase U.

Well, it’s good to see that Jacy Jayne was able to save Chase U. Now, NXT’s most entertaining faction can continue to focus on their future. Stay tuned to Ringside News for live coverage of NXT.
