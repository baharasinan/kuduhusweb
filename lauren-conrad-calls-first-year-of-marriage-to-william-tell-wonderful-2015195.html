Lauren Conrad Calls First Year of Marriage to William Tell &quot;Wonderful&quot;

Newlywed bliss! Lauren Conrad said “I do” to William Tell in September 2014 and she’s still in the honeymoon phase. The former Hills star told Us Weekly at the 10th Annual Women's Rights Awards in West Hollywood, Calif., on Monday, May 18, that being married to Tell has been “wonderful.”

“I haven’t gotten any big surprises yet. It’s been wonderful so far,” the fashion designer told Us about her first year of marriage.

“I think it’s really different for everybody,” she added when asked if being married feels different for her and her musician-turned-lawyer love. “For us it makes you officially family, which is really great, but as far as our relationship, we were really committed before so it didn't change a ton.”
