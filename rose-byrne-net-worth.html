FAQs About Rose Byrne

Who is Rose Byrne?

Rose Byrne is an Australian actress known for her roles in both television and film. She gained recognition for her role as Ellen Parsons on the TV series Damages and has appeared in films like Bridesmaids, Spy, X-Men: First Class, and Insidious.

When was she born?

She was born on July 24, 1979, in Balmain, a suburb of Sydney, Australia.

What was her first acting role?

She made her acting debut at the age of 15 in the film Dallas Doll in 1994. She continued to work in Australian television and film throughout the 1990s.

Has she won any awards?

Yes, she won the Volpi Cup for Best Actress at the Venice Film Festival for her role in The Goddess of 1967. She has also been nominated for two Golden Globe Awards and two Primetime Emmy Awards for her role in Damages.

What are some of her notable film roles?

Some of her notable film roles include Helen in Bridesmaids, Kelly Radner in Neighbors, Renai Lambert in Insidious, and Moira MacTaggert in X-Men: First Class.

Is she married?

She is not married, but she has been in a long-term relationship with actor Bobby Cannavale since 2012. They have two children together.
