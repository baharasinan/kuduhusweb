What is Tyler Dale's Net Worth?

Tyler Dale is an American reality television personality who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Tyler Dale is based in Las Vegas, Nevada, where he works in his father's antique restoration company, Rick's Restorations. He is slowly being groomed to take over the business from his father. Rick's Restorations is the subject of the reality series, "American Restoration" on the History Channel. The show follows the staff of Rick's Restorations as they go about restoring vintage and antique items. The series is a spin-off of the popular program, "Pawn Stars". Rick Dale began appearing on "Pawn Stars" during its first season, and appeared nine more times over the years restoring items for the main cast of "Pawn Stars". He was offered his own series in 2010. "American Restoration" is currently in its 4th season, and the cast includes Tyler Dale, Rick Dale, Tyler's uncle Ron, and his stepbrother, Brettly.
