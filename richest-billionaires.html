What is Yan Bin's Net Worth?

Yan Bin is a Thai-Chinese businessman who has a net worth of $10 billion dollars. Yan Bin was born in 1957 in Shandong Province in East China. Yan Bin founded the Reignwood Group in Thailand in 1984, a business conglomerate with interests in a range of consumer discretionary products and services such as energy drinks (Red Bull, specifically), golf courses, financial services, art and culture. Yan now serves as chairman of the group.

The company's initial activities were in general trading and contracting, as well as real estate development and tourism. In 1995, Yan expanded operations to China. His company has become a multinational enterprise in four industrial sectors. Reignwood has offshoots in Singapore, Canada, United States, United Kingdom, Germany and Switzerland.
