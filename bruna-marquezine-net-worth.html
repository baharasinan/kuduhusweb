Bruna Marquezine Net Worth: How Much Is Bruna Marquezine Worth?

Bruna Marquezine net worth-Brazilian actress, Bruna Reis Maia was born on August 4, 1995.

Marquezine was born in Duque de Caxias in the Baixada Fluminense, suburb of Rio de Janeiro in Brazil to Neide Maia and Telmo Maia. She shares the same parents with her sister, Luana Marquezine.

She took the last name Marquezine, which is Marchesini in Portuguese, in honor of her Italian-born paternal grandmother.

Bruna Marquezine career

Marquezine started her career on television in 2000 as one of the kids’ interviewees for the Márcio Garcia-hosted kids’ show Gente Inocente.

Bruna’s first acting role, however, was in a Military Police of So Paulo advertisement that addressed suicide and suicide prevention when she was just five years old.

Manoel Carlos and Ricardo Waddington saw this material and later asked her to appear in a soap drama. In 2003, Bruna made her telenovela debut in Manoel Carlos’ Mulheres Apaixonadas, where she gained widespread recognition for her portrayal as Salete.

She took up the role of Maria in the movie Xuxa Abracadabra that same year. The teenage actress joined the cast of the telenovela América in 2005, portraying Maria Flor, a blind character.

In 2006’s Cobras & Lagartos, the actress played the role of Lurdinha; in 2007’s Desejo Proibido, she played Maria Augusta.

She was promoted to the position of teen actress in 2008, and in the drama Negócio da China, she portrayed her first protagonist, the martial artist Flor de Lys.

She played the orphan Terezinha in the 2010 telenovela Araguaia. She joined the Quero Beijo cast in 2011 and plays the co-protagonist Belezinha, a beauty queen.

Marquezine performed the role of Lurdinha in the Salve Jorge soap drama in 2012. The actress claims that she thought about quitting acting at the same time due of the objectification of her body.

She accepted the author Manoel Carlos’ invitation to portray Helena, a recurring role in the second season of the soap opera Em Famlia, in 2014.

She appeared in her first broadcast naked scenes in the 2016 series Nada Será Como Antes, in which she played the dancer and aspiring actress Beatriz dos Santos.

Marquezine referred to the job as “the most challenging role of her career” at the time. She was a cast member of the 2018 soap drama Deus Salve o Rei.

A narrative with a medieval theme featured Catherine de Lurton, a villain for whom the actress had to shed five kg to fit the part.

In the Klaus Mitteldorf film Vou Nadar até Você, which played at the 47th Gramado Film Festival in 2019, she made her acting debut as a protagonist, bringing Ophelia to life.

After 17 years with TV Globo, she did not have her contract renewed in January 2020 since the network was implementing a new model of contracting by work and only keeping a small number of names in the fixed cast, the majority of whom were veterans.

Additionally, she taped a cameo in the Keanu Reeves-produced Netflix series Conquest. She and her buddy Manu Gavassi presented MTV Miaw in September, and soon after that she unveiled a YouTube channel to share the behind-the-scenes of her endeavors.

Marquezine was introduced by Netflix as the streaming service’s new hire on November 23, 2020. The next week, the production business said that the actress would star in the television series Maldivas as Liz, with performers including Manu Gavassi, Sheron Menezzes, and Klebber Toledo.

In an interview in November 2021, Bruna said that she had tried out for the role of Supergirl in The Flash and placed second.

Since she couldn’t travel to the final round, the pandemic was one of the reasons for the rejection.
