Early Life

Leslie Hope was born on May 29, 1903, in Eltham, London, United Kingdom. His father, William Henry Hope, was a talented stonemason, and his mother, Avis Townes, was a singer mainly into opera. When Bob Hope was four years old, he immigrated with his family to the United States and settled in Cleveland, Ohio. He had six brothers, producer Jack Hope, businessman Ivor Hope, George Hope, actor James Hope, and Frederick Hope, but most of them died.

Bob Hope was grown and raised in Cleveland, where he attended the Boys’ Industrial School and went to a dance school later.

Personal life

Bob Hope was first married to Grace Louise Troxell. She was a performer of a theatrical genre called Vaudeville. They got married for one year only from 1933-1934. Then, Bob married the singer Dolores Hope. Bob and Dolores adopted two sons and two daughters, Anthony J. Hope and actor William Kelly Francis Hope, Eleanora Hope, and producer Linda Hope. Dolores Hope also died in 2011.

On July 27, 2003, at his home in Toluca Lake, Los Angeles, Bob Hope died because of pneumonia. (100 years old)
