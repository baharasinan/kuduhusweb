Edward Furlong Biography

In September 1992, Edward fell in love with Jacqueline Domac. Jacqueline Domac is a former tutor. After they began dating each other, she acted as his manager. She served in this post for several years and then finally in May 1999, they announced their separation. She criticized him for assaulting her and she sued him for 15% of his earnings.
