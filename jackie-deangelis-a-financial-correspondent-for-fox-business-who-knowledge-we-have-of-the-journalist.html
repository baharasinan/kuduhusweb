Since 2019, Jackie Deangelis has covered finance for the FOX Business Network. She updates the network’s New York headquarters on breaking business news.

Deangelis presented a unique five-day segment series on the escalating economic conflict between the two opposing ideologies during the capitalism vs. socialism town hall on FBN in May 2019. In August 2020, she spoke with Wilbur Ross, the American secretary of commerce.

Prior to joining FBN, Jackie spent 13 years working for CNBC, where she held the positions of network chief energy correspondent and host of the Futures Now online commodities show.

Deangelis produced market reports and covered the financial repercussions of important events throughout her time at CNBC, such as the protests against the Dakota Access Pipeline and Hurricanes Irma and Harvey.

During the years 2010 and 2011, Deangelis lived in the Middle East. She interviewed well-known business leaders for CNBC International, including OPEC Secretary General Abdalla El-Badri and Saudi Arabia’s Finance Minister Ibrahi Al-Assaf. In December 2018, DeAngelis left CNBC and joined Yahoo Finance, where she served as the host of the The Ticker lunchtime program.

In this role, Deangelis broke down the most significant financial stories of the day in addition to interviewing company leaders and subject matter experts such U.S. Shell President Gretchen Watkins and JPMorgan Chief Economist Anthony Chan.
