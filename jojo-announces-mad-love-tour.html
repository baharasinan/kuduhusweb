JoJo Announces The Mad Love Tour 2017

JoJo has announced dates for her Mad Love Tour in 2017. She will be hitting the UK and Europe before hitting 29 states of America.

Previously, today, she announced the UK & Ireland dates of this tour; however, with this update, there are added Europe dates and 46 US dates.

Check Out JoJo’s Mad Love Tour Dates here:

https://www.instagram.com/p/BNZxsG_BdKG

The UK and Europe tickets go on sale on 2 December at 9am. The US tickets go on sale on 9 December.

According to her website, there are more dates to be released, including a tour of Canada.

These dates were revealed after she dropped her new music video for second single “FAB.” which features Remy Ma.
